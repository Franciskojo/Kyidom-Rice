Our Story

Kyidom Foods is a product of Jireh-Aidoo Ltd, an agribusiness in Ghana that specializes in the production and distribution of authentic Ghanaian rice and rice products. Jireh-Aidoo Ltd was born in 2020 during the rice import halt, and Kyidom Foods has grown from serving family and neighbors to becoming a brand that empowers communities.

What started as a small operation in the Kasoa Otta City, Central Region has grown into one of Ghana's most trusted rice brands, known for its exceptional quality and commitment to community development.

Our Milestones

2020

Founded June 1st at Kasoa.

Started with 250kg bags from 1 farmer. Started packaging.

2021

Commercial packaging began.

Won Tony Elumelu Foundation Grant for expansion. Partnered with 3 farmers.

2022

Expanded distribution to 3 shops.

Worked with 5 farmers.

2023

Transitioned to a Limited Liability Company.

Brand name changed from Dinah to Kyidom. Trademark registered. Farmers grew to 20.

2024

Expanded distribution to 8 channels.

2025

Distribution in 10 shops (and counting).

Improved packaging. Partnered with 50 farmers directly and 200 indirectly.
