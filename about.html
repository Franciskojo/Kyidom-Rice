Our Story

Discover the journey behind Kyidom Rice

From Our Farms to Your Table

Founded in 2015, Kyidom Rice began with a simple mission: to provide Ghanaians with high-quality, locally grown rice while supporting smallholder farmers across the country.

What started as a small operation in the Ashanti Region has grown into one of Ghana's most trusted rice brands, known for its exceptional quality and commitment to community development.

Our Farming Process

We work directly with over 200 smallholder farmers across Ghana, providing them with training, resources, and fair prices for their harvest. Our rice is:

  • Grown using sustainable farming practices
  • Hand-harvested at peak ripeness
  • Carefully cleaned and sorted to remove all impurities
  • Packaged in hygienic facilities

Community Impact

For every bag of Kyidom Rice sold, we reinvest 5% of profits into:

  • Farmer education programs
  • Women's agricultural cooperatives
  • Youth farming initiatives
  • Community infrastructure projects
